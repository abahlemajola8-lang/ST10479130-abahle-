Get Involved

Donations

Donations are much valued and essential to the organization's ongoing growth, allowing us to consistently reach communities—the communities that most require our services. For the project that supports orphans and vulnerable children, we also require donations of school supplies and materials, such as school uniforms too help the ones in need. We greatly value every donation.

Monetary Donations

Giving liberally, whether one-time or on a regular basis, makes you an essential part of the Siphilisiwe Foundation of SA's fundamental effort to empower people and stop gender-based violence.Anything you are willing to provide supports our work to fight social injustices in our nation and encourage emotional wellness.
