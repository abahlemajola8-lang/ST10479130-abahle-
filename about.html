What we specialize in

Aims And Objectives

Siphilisiwe Foundation of South Africa is a non-profit organisation with the following aims and objectives: areas of service to the community

Primary Objectives

  • Responding to Gender Based Violence.
  • Eduction and Empowerment of AGYW.
  • Grooming young boy to become better men to curb the spread of Gender based violence
  • Providing therapeutic services to victims and their families
  • Providing trauma debriefing to professionals and families
  • Providing HIV&AIDS realted counselling, VCT (Voluntary Counselling and Testing), education, awareness, empowerment and other services in support of efforts to combat the spread of the disease and to empower those already infected to be able to lead productive lives
  • Life skills and empowerment training services in support of people in terms of community upliftment and capacity building especially children, women, youth, and other vulnerable populations
  • Providing training and capacity of professionals and community
  • Mentorship of small community based organisation
  • Supervision of Social Workers

The organisation's secondary objectives will be to:

  • To work in collaboration with other organisations and Government Department that mainly deal with professional work services
  • To collaborate with government departments for combat the spread of Gender Based Violence

Secondary Objectives

  • Welfare and humanitarian activities;
  • Provide treatment and contribute positively towards the research of HIV/AIDS treatment
  • Creating opportunities for employment including in-service training in therapeutic skills, so enabling them to make a positive contribution to society and to develop self-esteem and self-radiance
  • Ensuring maximum efficiency with respect to the accountability and administration of funds

Mission

To create a constitutional framework that protects human rights, promotes gender equality, provides access to comprehensive healthcare, educates the public, and establishes robust accountability systems in order to prevent and address HIV/AIDS and gender-based violence while empowering and supporting vulnerable groups.

Vision

A fair, inclusive, and healthy society in which everyone has equal access to protection, dignity, healthcare, education, and protection from the effects of HIV/AIDS and gender-based violence.
